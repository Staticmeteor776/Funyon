New Oregon Trail Experience!

Embark on an unforgettable adventure with the new Oregon Trail Experience, a modern reimagining of the classic pioneer journey. This immersive experience combines historical storytelling with interactive technology to transport participants back to the 1800s. Whether you're traveling by covered wagon, solving survival challenges, or making tough decisions on the trail, every moment is packed with excitement, education, and exploration. Set along a scenic outdoor path or experienced virtually through an app-enhanced journey, this new version of the trail brings history to life like never before.

The Oregon Trail Experience is more than just a journey—it's a chance to step into the shoes of early American pioneers and discover the challenges they faced. Whether you're a history buff, an educator, or just looking for a fun day out with the family, this adventure has something for everyone. With captivating storytelling and hands-on activities, it's both entertaining and educational. Our staff is passionate about making your experience memorable, safe, and inspiring. Don’t miss out on this unique opportunity to walk the trail and create your own story along the way. Come visit us today and start your Oregon Trail adventure!
